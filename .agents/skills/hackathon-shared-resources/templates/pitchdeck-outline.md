**Type:** Solution
**Time budget:** 15–20 seconds

**Content:**

- "[Project Name] is a [type of product] that [action] for [user] so that [outcome]."
- 2–3 key differentiators
- One visual or screenshot

**Speaker note:**

> [Transition: "Let me show you how it works."]

## Slide 5: How It Works (Technology)

**Type:** Technology
**Time budget:** 15–20 seconds

**Content:**

- Architecture diagram (simple) OR tech stack list
- Key technical decision or innovation
- Sponsor API or prize track technology highlighted

**Speaker note:**

> [Keep it brief. Technical credibility, not a lecture.]

---

## Slide 6: Impact / Vision

**Type:** Vision
**Time budget:** 15–20 seconds

**Content:**

- Scale of potential impact (users, markets, outcomes)
- "What if" framing: what the world looks like if this succeeds
- Connection from MVP to 10x vision

**Speaker note:**

> [This is the aspirational moment. Let it land.]

---

## Slide 7: Team

**Type:** Team
**Time budget:** 10–15 seconds

**Content:**

- Team member names and roles
- One "why us" sentence: lived experience or relevant expertise
- Team photo (optional but memorable)

**Speaker note:**

> [Be confident. You solved this because you cared.]

---

## Slide 8: Call to Action / Close

**Type:** CTA
**Time budget:** 10 seconds

**Content:**

- [Memorable closing statement — not "thank you"]
- Options: a question for judges, a vision statement, or a challenge

**Speaker note:**

> [End on energy. The last thing said is the most remembered.]

## Slide Design Notes

- Maximum 7 words per bullet point
- One idea per slide
- Minimum 24pt font
- High contrast (dark background or light background — not gray)
- Project name visible on every slide (footer or watermark)
- No clip art; use screenshots, diagrams, or solid color backgrounds
